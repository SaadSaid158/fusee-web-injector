Instructions

  1. Put your Switch in RCM mode using a jig or modchip.
  2. Connect your Switch to your computer via your USB-C cable
  3. Click "Connect Switch" and select "APX" in the device list.
  4. Choose a payload (either predefined or custom .bin file) and click "Inject Payload"
  5. If you have any issues, please consult this guide.
